A little bit about me.

Home base, for me, is wherever my family is. My husband's career has us moving all over the country and beyond so home really is where the heart is. My journey into tech started with an article in the WSJ about Mom's starting a career in their 40's. I was fascinated with the idea of having a career at this point in my life and not only that but a portable career that I can grow no matter where my family goes.

I took my first class with Skillcrush and I was hooked! That curiosity then opened a door that could never be shut. When I learned how to build my first website, I realized I found something that gave me the freedom & versatility I was looking for in my work. Now I am utilizing my new skills to code classic and stylish websites.
